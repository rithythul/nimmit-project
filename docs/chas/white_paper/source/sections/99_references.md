# References

**Coase, R. H. (1937).** *The Nature of the Firm.* Economica, 4(16), 386-405.  
> Foundation for Transaction Cost Economics. Explains why companies exist and the "hidden costs" of management.

**Licklider, J. C. R. (1960).** *Man-Computer Symbiosis.* IRE Transactions on Human Factors in Electronics, HFE-1, 4-11.  
> Early vision of human-AI partnership. Predicted the "very tight coupling" of human brains and computing machines.

**Wegner, D. M. (1985).** *Transactive Memory: A Contemporary Analysis of the Group Mind.* In B. Mullen & G. R. Goethals (Eds.), Theories of Group Behavior (pp. 185-208). Springer-Verlag.  
> Concept of how groups store and retrieve information collectively. The academic basis for the "Context Cloud."

**Halbwachs, M. (1925).** *Les cadres sociaux de la mémoire.* Paris: Félix Alcan.  
> Early research on collective and institutional memory.

**Lazzarato, M. (1996).** *Immaterial Labour.* In P. Virno & M. Hardt (Eds.), Radical Thought in Italy: A Potential Politics (pp. 133-147). University of Minnesota Press.  
> Analysis of "immaterial" tasks (information, style, and communication) in the modern economy.

**Simondon, G. (1958).** *Du mode d'existence des objets techniques.* Paris: Aubier.  
> Philosophical analysis of the relationship between humans and technical objects (precedent for "Agentic Pod" dynamics).
